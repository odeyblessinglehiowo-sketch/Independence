About This Project

This website was developed by participants of the G4EP Digital Training under the R.I.S.E Project as part of a collaborative clas s project, to commemorate Nigeria’s Independence. It documents and celebrates the significant contributions of Nigerian women from the pre-independence era through the attainment of sovereignty and into the contemporary period. By presenting carefully curated narratives, images, and profiles, the project seeks to honor the resilience, leadership, and enduring impact of women whose efforts have shaped the nation’s history, and to inspire future generations to build upon their legacy.
